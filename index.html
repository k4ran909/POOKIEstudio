About VIRELIA

Virelia: Echoes of the Hollow Bloom is an epic, story-driven survival adventure that takes players on a journey across the wild, the fallen, and the unknown. You begin as a lone pilot who survives a devastating helicopter crash deep in the uncharted jungle, where danger lurks in every shadow. With the help of a loyal monkey companion, you must fight for survival against hostile creatures, face the violence of nature, and uncover the mystery of a divided city—one half a neon-lit cyberpunk metropolis chasing progress, the other a crumbling wasteland abandoned to time. But the journey doesn’t stop there; beyond the ruins of Earth, Virelia ventures into the stars, aboard the International Space Station and the revolutionary ISS 2.0, where humanity’s future hangs in the balance.


Developed single-handedly under Pookie Studios and powered by Unreal Engine 5.6, Virelia is crafted to deliver breathtakingly realistic visuals, vast open worlds, and a story that blends survival, resilience, and human connection. More than just a game, it’s an emotional odyssey—challenging players not only to survive but to endure, to hope, and to dream of a new future beyond the hollow bloom.
